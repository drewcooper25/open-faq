BIG OPEN Survey FAQs

Frequently asked questions regarding our Big OPEN Survey

What are you trying to find out with your study?

We want to find out if the use of do-it-yourself artificial pancreas systems (DIYAPS; open source loop systems) has an influence on the health and well-being of people with diabetes. We want to examine the challenges associated with building DIYAPS, and why some people choose not to use DIYAPS. And we aim to identify potential technical improvements that could be made to artificial technology of all kinds.
